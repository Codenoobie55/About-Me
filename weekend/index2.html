About James- The man, myth and legend

James Branca
A bit of a globe-trotter, James was born in New Haven Connecticut and grew to love the allure of the ocean, outdoors and his fellow human brothers and sisters. He spent time back-packing in SouthEast Asia and Europe before deciding to serve his country in the United States Navy as a PhotoJournalist. While in Asia he grew to love kind-hearted attitude of the Thai locals on the island of Koh Tao where he fell in love with sport diving. After the military he decided to attend the Diver's Institute of Technology in Seattle. Now, he is immersing himself into a new lifestyle at Code Fellows where his is learning coding, soft skills and how Unbutu is the shiit!!
